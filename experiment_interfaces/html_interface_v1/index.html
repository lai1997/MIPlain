In this experiment you will show your ability to play and learn the game, in particular by picking your
4th, 3rd and 2nd move on a randomly chosen board and reflect on your choices while being provided with some feedback.

This experiment includes at most 7 stages, each could be either a training or testing phase.
You will answer some questions in each phase.

For each QUESTION, you should submit what you think to be the optimal move by clicking
on the board and pressing 'Proceed'. You will receive feedback in training phase after
submitting your choice only when you make a mistake.

NOTE: Each question is timed. When time runs out, you will be forced to proceed.
At the end of your session, you will download a record file. Please follow the instructions given.
